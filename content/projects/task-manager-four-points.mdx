- Implemented mobile-responsive design for on-the-go task management

The AI-powered WhatsApp Task Manager revolutionizes hotel operations by combining the convenience of WhatsApp communication with powerful AI capabilities. Staff can manage tasks through natural language commands via WhatsApp, while the AI system intelligently processes, prioritizes, and assigns tasks. The integration with Chatwoot enables seamless communication flow, while OpenAI powers natural language understanding and automated responses, significantly improving operational efficiency and staff coordination.
