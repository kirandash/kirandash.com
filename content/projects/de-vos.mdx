- Store Management

  - Opening hours and contact details

- Product Catalog

  - Dynamic product listings
  - Category-based organization
  - Product search and filtering
  - Detailed product specifications

- Telecom Services

  - Mobile plan comparisons
  - Service package details
  - Online subscription options
  - Coverage area information

- Content Management

  - Custom WordPress dashboard
  - Role-based access control
  - Media library management
  - SEO optimization tools

- Technical Features
  - Responsive design across devices
  - Fast page load times
  - Cross-browser compatibility
  - Custom WordPress plugins
  - Advanced caching system

The De Vos website serves as a comprehensive platform for the company's retail and telecom operations. It combines an intuitive product catalog with store management features, making it easy for customers to find products and services in their area. The multilingual support ensures accessibility for both Dutch and international customers, while the custom WordPress implementation provides efficient content management for the business team. The platform's responsive design and optimized performance deliver a seamless experience across all devices.
